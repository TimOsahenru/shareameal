Our core values

We are a People of Faith: Our Christian faith compels us to respond to the needs of orphaned, vulnerable, and disadvantaged people. We believe that God would transform their lives in partnership with our actions.

We are Accountable: We account for our activities, accept responsibility for them, and transparently share results.

About Us

Fighting Hunger, One Meal at a Time

Share the Meal Nigeria The Share A Meal Charitable Foundation was birthed in January 2020 after the Founder, Pastor Dagogo Pollyn, the President of Dagogo Pollyn Foundation, made a profound statement “When You Help The Less Privilege, You Are Giving To God”. The Share A Meal Charitable Foundation is a faith-based, Humanitarian-Centered, Non-Governmental Organization in consultative status with the United Nations Economic and Social Council. We are registered in Nigeria with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) Abuja, RC No CAC/IT/NO ...... We Believe in the future of the indigent, less privileged and we take action to reach and meet their needs. This is the CORE of our work. Over the years, our programs and interventions have impacted the lives of so many indigent beneficiaries in hard-to-reach communities. Our mission

What our mission is as an NGO

To create an environment where the less privileged, lives above poverty, and becomes an active participant in socio-economic development irrespective of race, color, or gender. Through our Education, Food, Livelihood, Healthcare, Advocacy, Shelter, and Humanitarian Aid programs, we strive to ensure poor families succeed in life by providing them with Christ-centered care and sustained solutions to issues caused by poverty.
