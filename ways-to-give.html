Ways to Give

Your generosity fuels our mission to fight hunger and change lives

Donate Now

Give meals and hope to a family in need. Every ₦1,000 provides up to 5 meals for a family in need.

Your Impact:

  • ₦10,000 feeds a family for a week
  • ₦50,000 provides a month of meals
  • ₦100,000 supports emergency food relief

Planned Giving

Leave a legacy that fights hunger for generations to come

A planned gift to Share A Meal Charitable Foundation is more than a donation—it's a promise to care for future generations. Your gift will provide meals, dignity, and hope to regions in need across all nations.

A gift in your will is a simple yet meaningful way to support our mission to fight hunger long into the future. You can complete your gift by including just one sentence in your will.

Sample Bequest Language:

"I give, devise, and bequeath to Share A Meal Charitable Foundation, a nonprofit organization located at [address], the sum of $____ or ____% of my estate to be used for its general charitable purposes."

Name Share A Meal Charitable Foundation as a beneficiary of retirement accounts, life insurance policies, or commercial annuities.

  1. Request a change-of-beneficiary form from your plan administrator
  2. Choose what percentage (1%-100%) to leave to Share A Meal
  3. Return the completed form to your provider

Receive fixed payments for life while supporting our mission. A charitable gift annuity provides:

  • Fixed payments for life (based on your age)
  • Potential tax benefits
  • The satisfaction of making a lasting impact
