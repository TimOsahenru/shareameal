The Birth of Share A Meal

The Share A Meal Charitable Foundation was birthed in January 2020 after the Founder, Pastor Dagogo Pollyn, the President of Dagogo Pollyn Foundation, made a profound statement: "When You Help The Less Privileged, You Are Giving To God".

The Share A Meal Charitable Foundation is a faith-based, Humanitarian-Centered, Non-Governmental Organization in consultative status with the United Nations Economic and Social Council. We are registered in Nigeria with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) Abuja.

We Believe in the future of the indigent, less privileged and we take action to reach and meet their needs. This is the CORE of our work.

Over the years, our programs and interventions have impacted the lives of so many indigent beneficiaries in hard-to-reach communities.

Our Purpose

Guided by Faith & Compassion

Our Mission

To create an environment where the less privileged lives above poverty, and becomes an active participant in socio-economic development irrespective of race, color, or gender. Through our Education, Food, Livelihood, Healthcare, Advocacy, Shelter, and Humanitarian Aid programs, we strive to ensure poor families succeed in life by providing them with Christ-centered care and sustained solutions to issues caused by poverty.

Our Vision

We envision a world, free of poverty, where every human has equal opportunities to thrive and fulfill their God-given potential.

Core Values

The Principles That Guide Us

People of Faith

Our Christian faith compels us to respond to the needs of orphaned, vulnerable, and disadvantaged people. We believe that God would transform their lives in partnership with our actions.

Community of Hope

We are confident of our expectations that everyone we reach will rise to their full potential and realize God's purpose in their lives. This inspires and drives us daily to bring hope to our world.

Accountability

We account for our activities, accept responsibility for them, and transparently share results with our stakeholders and donors.

Servants of Grace

We show love regardless of color, race, religion, economic status, and gender. We believe that to bring about the change we seek, we must demonstrate unconditional love.

Authentic Relationships

We are dedicated to developing genuine partnerships and relationships based on trust with communities, donors, and partners.

Respect & Integrity

We value each individual and model honesty in all donor interactions, maintaining the highest ethical standards in all we do.
